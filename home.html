Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Vantrix Global.

How quickly can you fill a position?

Our average placement time is 48 hours from requirement submission to shortlisted candidates. For specialized roles, we maintain a pre-vetted pool ready to start immediately.

+

What's your replacement guarantee?

If a hired professional doesn't work out within the first 30 days, we replace them for free. After 30 days, we offer pro-rated credits for replacements within 90 days.

+

How do you vet professionals?

Multi-stage process: resume review, technical assessment, coding interview, background check, reference verification, and culture fit evaluation. Only top 5% pass all stages.

+

What payment models do you offer?

Flexible options: hourly rates for contractors, fixed monthly fees for contract roles, and placement fees for permanent hires. No hidden costs or long-term contracts required.

+

Can professionals work with multiple clients?

Yes. Our professionals can take on multiple projects simultaneously if it aligns with their availability and your needs. Most prefer full-time roles but flexibility is available.

+

How do I get started as a professional?

Sign up and create your profile (5 minutes), pass our vetting process (1-2 weeks), then start receiving opportunities. Most professionals are matched within the first month.

+

What locations do you hire in?

Global coverage across 50+ countries. We handle all legal, tax, and compliance requirements for each jurisdiction. Fully remote work is standard across our network.

+

Do you handle legal and compliance?

Completely. We manage contracts, employment agreements, tax compliance, NDA enforcement, and all legal documentation. You work with professionals, we handle the paperwork.
